Lilies – The Elegant Bloom

❁❁❁
❁❁❁

Lilies are timeless symbols of beauty, grace, and elegance. From wedding bouquets to peaceful gardens, their fragrant petals and regal form have captured hearts across the world.

🌷 Types of Lilies

  • Asiatic Lilies – Bright and bold
  • Oriental Lilies – Large and fragrant
  • Trumpet Lilies – Classic, trumpet-shaped blooms
  • Tiger Lilies – Orange with dark speckles
  • Calla Lilies – Sleek, elegant (not true lilies)

💮 Symbolism

Lilies often represent purity, devotion, and renewal. In many cultures, white lilies symbolize innocence, while pink and red shades represent love and admiration.

🌿 Growing Conditions

These perennial beauties thrive in full to partial sun with well-drained soil. With love and care, they reward gardeners with a stunning summer display.

🌼 Fun Fact

Some lilies are so fragrant that just a few stems can perfume an entire room. However, be cautious—many are toxic to cats!
